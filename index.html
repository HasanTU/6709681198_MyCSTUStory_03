Personal Portfolio of Dekshine San

CSTU

     I am someone who enjoys innovations related to modern technology. I have a keen interest in technology and am observant by nature. My curiosity for discovering new things and my ability to observe have allowed me to recognize various problems within my own community. These issues include educational challenges and the lack of opportunities for underprivileged children. These problems persist around me, and over time, I’ve set a life goal for myself: to bring about change here, to lead opportunities to this place, because this is my hometown.
     I’ve asked myself what I truly want to be. This led me to explore my own identity by participating in various activities. Eventually, I joined project competitions related to applying technology, whether it’s Artificial Intelligence, Arduino, or developing different applications. These experiences have ignited my passion and fulfilled my dreams. Due to the lack of institutions teaching these skills in my province, I’ve had to self-study through online resources like Google, YouTube, and even by asking questions in programming groups on Facebook. Despite occasional obstacles in my learning journey, I’ve never given up. I continue to seek knowledge and hone my skills. I always tell myself that I won’t stop here—I’ll keep developing myself. My ultimate goal is to contribute to the growth of Narathiwat, my hometown, whether it’s through educational opportunities or other means.
     And why Thammasat? Because Thammasat is the university of giving and opportunity. Thammasat empowers people, encourages them to do what’s right, and provides freedom—both in expression and attire. Even though it grants freedom, it remains within the boundaries of what is just.

"Thammasat is the university of the people"
